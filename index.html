Frequently asked questions

What AI models does Playlab support?▼

Playlab gives you access to 8+ leading AI models including ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Llama, and more — all through a single, school-safe interface with enterprise-grade data privacy.

How does pilot pricing work?▼

Most partners start with a smaller pilot or cohort and receive 50–90% discounted pricing in Years 1–2 as adoption ramps. We work with you to design a rollout that fits your budget and timeline.

Is Playlab safe for students?▼

Yes. Playlab has been vetted by NYC Public Schools and California Community Colleges. We feature best-in-class AI safety with agentic moderation, content filtering, and full admin oversight.

Can we integrate with our existing LMS?▼

Absolutely. Scaling and Transforming plans include SSO/LMS integration, Clever rostering, and Canvas LMS integration. We support most major education platforms.

What makes Playlab different from MagicSchool?▼

Playlab is a nonprofit committed to open, accessible AI for education. We offer access to 8+ AI models (not just one), dedicated learning engineering support, and a platform designed for creating custom AI apps — not just using pre-built templates.
